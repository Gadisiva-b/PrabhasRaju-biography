career

Full Name: Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju Date of Birth: 23 October 1979 Birthplace: Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu, India Education: He earned a Bachelor of Technology (BTech) degree from Sri Chaitanya College, Hyderabad. Family: His father is Uppalapati Surya Narayana Raju and his uncle was the veteran Telugu actor Krishnam Raju. Early Career (2002 2004) Debut: Eeswar (2002) Early films: Raghavendra (2003) Breakthrough: Varsham (2004) Big commercial success. Growth as a Lead Actor (2005 2013) Prabhas delivered several action and romantic hits: Chatrapathi (2005) Established him as an action star. Billa (2009) Stylish action entertainer. Darling (2010) Romantic success. Mr. Perfect (2011) Major family hit. Mirchi (2013) Award-winning performance. Pan-India Stardom (2015 Present) Baahubali series changed Indian cinema: Baahubali: The Beginning (2015) Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017) One of the highest-grossing Indian films. After Baahubali, Prabhas became a Pan-India superstar and acted in high-budget films: Saaho (2019) Radhe Shyam (2022) Adipurush (2023) Salaar (2023) Kalki 2898 AD (2024)

achievements

Awards & Recognition Won the Nandi Award for Best Actor for Mirchi (2013). Won CineMAA Award Best Actor (Critics) for Darling. Won SIIMA Award Best Actor (Telugu) for Baahubali 2: The Conclusion. Multiple Filmfare South nominations (Telugu) for several films. Box-Office / Recordbreaking Achievements Baahubali 2: The Conclusion was one of the first Indian films to gross over ₹1,000 crore worldwide. He is reportedly the first Indian actor to have two films gross over ₹1,000 crore: Baahubali 2 and Kalki 2898 AD. He has six films that opened with a worldwide gross of over ₹100 crore. He is the only South Indian actor with six films crossing ₹100 crore nett in the Hindi market. Milestones & Firsts First South Indian actor to have a wax statue at Madame Tussauds. Regarded as one of the first true pan-India superstars, thanks to Baahubali's success and nationwide appeal. Philanthropy He has donated to flood relief efforts (e.g., Andhra Pradesh / Telangana) and contributed during the COVID-19 pandemic. He has pledged to support education (according to some reports) by helping cover school fees for children. Industry Impact Helped transform the Telugu film industry into a pan-India force — Baahubali's success raised the bar for scale and box-office potential for South Indian cinema. According to Forbes (2025), he is one of the highest-paid actors in India, commanding fees in the ₹100-200 crore range per film.
